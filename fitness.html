5 Tips to Keep Your Fitness Journey on Point

1. Set Realistic Goals

Start with achievable goals to stay motivated. For example, aim to work out 3 times a week or lose 1-2 pounds per month.

2. Stay Consistent

Consistency is key. Even small, regular efforts lead to big results over time.

3. Mix It Up

Vary your workouts to avoid boredom and target different muscle groups.

4. Track Your Progress

Keep a journal or use an app to monitor your workouts, diet, and achievements.

5. Rest and Recover

Your body needs time to heal. Make sure to get enough sleep and take rest days.
